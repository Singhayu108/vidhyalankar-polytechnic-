VISION AND MISSION

Vision:

To produce Electronics and Computer engineers capable of effectively using technical knowledge and interpersonal skills to benefit the industry and society.

Mission:

M1: Providing state of the art facilities and conducive environment enabling the students to sustain the challenges in the field of Electronics and Computer.

M2: Educating the students to face the competitive world, develop leadership skills and to instil discipline and ethics.

M3: Promoting industry institute interaction.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

Core Competence:

To develop expertise amongst students to meet the needs of the employer by using mathematical foundation, electronic fundamentals, and enable them to understand and solve engineering problems.

Professionalism:

To inculcate life-long learning, courage, professional ethics, and entrepreneurial mindset.

Conducive Learning Environment:

To provide an encouraging academic learning environment needed for a successful professional career so that students can become a resourceful asset to society.

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES

PSO1: Technical Skills

Apply knowledge of electronics and computer engineering to analyze, design, and implement electronic systems and computer applications.

PSO2: Professional Skills

Demonstrate proficiency in using modern electronic tools, software, and equipment for professional practice.

PSO3: Project Management

Work effectively in teams to design and develop innovative solutions for real-world engineering problems.

PROGRAMME OUTCOMES

PO1: Engineering Knowledge

Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals to solve complex engineering problems.

PO2: Problem Analysis

Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems.

PO3: Design/Development

Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes.

About EJ Department

Electronics and Computer engineering is the most sophisticated, demanding and largest branch of engineering. It has diverse application areas that have affected our daily life in various ways. The world is anticipating a new millennium, which is going to be the wireless telecommunication age. Therefore new areas of research in material sciences, optics, MEMS, NEMS, together will contribute to a new revolution in replacing the present state of art devices and systems. Vidyalankar Polytechnic offers a three year Diploma course in Electronics and Computer Engineering affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai and Approved by All India Council for Technical Education.
